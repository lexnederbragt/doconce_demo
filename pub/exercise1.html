Exercise 2: my second exercise

Here goes a general introduction to the exercise.

a) Implement this difference equation in Python and generate a plot for values of \( x \) from 1 to 10.

$$ \begin{equation} x_{n+1} = 1.5 x_n -20 x_0 = 20 \label{_auto1} \end{equation} $$

Solution. 

from matplotlib import pyplot as plt
x = [20]
i = 1
while i < 10:
    x.append(1.5 * x[i-1] - 20)
    i = i + 1

plt.plot(x)
plt.show()
